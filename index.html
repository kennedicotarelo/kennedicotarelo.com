about

experiments, and fragments of ideas—some complete, some still unfolding. each project is part exploration, part expression, built inside the systems of games and gently pushed beyond them.

i primarily create games and mods, alongside writing in areas such as storytelling and philosophy. i also have experience in directing from past projects, as well as a strong background in audio engineering. earlier, i worked extensively with web and programming including c#, c++, and LUA, although my current focus has shifted more toward creative work. overall i love creating feelings through experiences, originally since age of seven.
