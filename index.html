about

i'm a video game developer/modder, director, writer and composer

experiments, and fragments of ideas—some complete, some still unfolding.

each project is part exploration, part expression, built inside the systems of games
and gently pushed beyond them.

i primarily create games and mods, alongside writing in areas such as storytelling and philosophy.
i've had experiences in directing from past projects, as well as a strong background
in audio engineering, composing. i worked extensively with coding including c#, c++, and LUA.

overall i love creating feelings through experiences, it has been my passion since age of seven.



sharing my 'art' and art should stay as it is, not as a product.
