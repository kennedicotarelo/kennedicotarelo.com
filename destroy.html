The year is 2008, years after Black Mesa incident. HECU has gone international with its 'cleaning' mission.

Xen creatures are wiped off from the surface, for now. The time has come for control. With all troops gone rouge, this had lead to the creation of counter-international defence agency called DESTROY.

You—the best of the best who also escaped from the incident way back are qualified for securing and extracting. Kill anything that gets in your way.


Destroy is fast-paced unforgiving combat game of a former mod series that takes place in a eastern country years after the events in Half-Life.
