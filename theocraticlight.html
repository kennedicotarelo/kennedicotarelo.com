Theocratic Light
status: WIP

Theocratic Light is narritive story about letting go of your past.

No matter how painful the memories you have, eventually you'll have to let go, or drown in the past. Try to find comfort in the past's shining light on present. Let them remain as a memory.


The protagonist returns to their childhood place after long set of years. Reliving the past memories, but the memories will become hostile. Manipulating echos from the past while walking and reminiscing, the protagonist have to resist the past's infulence.


work in progress.
