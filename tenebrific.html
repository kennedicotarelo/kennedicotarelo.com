Tenebrific
release: unknown

"No one else will come for you. Sacrifices are necessary, but do they deserve forgiveness, or this life's simply not worth living? You can't save everyone."

A young woman who finds herself in the middle of the madness after an unknown impact which was about to last night long.

After living a life in solitude, something was noticable with them. Seeing all the people killing each other all around the world made the choice solitude seem more acceptable. But that wasn't a real choice. Eventually it found you. After the impact, you get conscious from a familiar edge, where you can see your home. Aimless in what to do, you can only think of going back to home. As you wonder among the silent streets, the sun finally disappears, leaving you with darkness and unknown fate that's gonna last a night long. You can only rely in your butterfly knife that's been gifted to you.


A GoldSrc engine psychological horror game as a teenage girl going through the human-driven madness from one part to other part of the city, fighting with scarce resources, unexperienced in defence. Completely alone. Against humans, not monsters. But aren't they already?
